SPORTS

Sports can be defined as physical activities that involve competition, skill development, and physical fitness. Sports can benefit students in many ways, including promoting physical health, developing teamwork and leadership skills, enhancing social skills, building confidence and self-esteem, and providing opportunities for personal growth and achievement. Participation of Sports in HGIC

At SOS HGIC, sports play an important role in promoting a well-rounded education for students. Through participating in sports programs, students have the opportunity to develop their physical fitness, build strong relationships with peers, and learn important skills such as time management and goal setting. Additionally, sports can help students develop a strong sense of discipline, perseverance, and determination, which can translate to success in other areas of their lives.
